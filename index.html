MUSEUM OF CANDY

The Museum of Candy is an exciting attraction designed for candy enthusiasts of all ages, offering a unique and immersive experience. Here are detailed insights based on available information:

NEW YORK CITY

Another Museum of Candy was set to open in New York City in the summer of 2025, offering a permanent fixture for candy lovers. This museum includes 15 different candy-themed rooms and a dessert marketplace

DUBAI

Located on Umm Hurair Rd, Dubai, this is the world's first candy museum, which opened its doors on October 31, 2024. It's designed to be an interactive and playful space where visitors can indulge in a sugary adventure.
